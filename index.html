About
Tabriz Trend is a social and news media platform based in the city of Tabriz.

We focus on sharing the latest news, events, culture, tourism, and everyday happenings of Tabriz and Iranian Azerbaijan.

Our main activities are on Instagram and Telegram, where we introduce the beauty of Tabriz, highlight its traditions and customs, and provide up‑to‑date social content.

With a commitment to accurate reporting and cultural representation, Tabriz Trend aims to showcase the unique identity of our city.
